Hand-In One VR in the browser!























For Hand-In 1, I have created a small island with Nintendo-related characters - Five Pokémon & Spyro. Utilizing the animation-property in A-Frame, I have created some fitting animations for the Pokémons: Mew & Ditto, and I have varied the intensity and colors on some of the different lights in the scene One of each A-Frame-light-type is added to the scene, and multiple objects cast shadows. The atmosphere changes between a late evening to early night, and raindrops are added using a particle-system-component made by IdeaSpaceVR, and a linear rain-sound-file is added to enhance the ambience. The water is made using a simple A-Frame circle, with an added custom-shader to give the water a caustic effect.
